ABOUT THE PRIVATE OFFICE

Unrivalled business connections

With unrivalled business connections, we foster strategic partnerships at the highest level, helping your company to excel. We invest in and partner with leading global companies across a diverse portfolio of industries, leveraging The Private Office management team’s wealth of expertise.

After identifying potential partners, we present them to an Executive Committee for approval. If successful, our International Ventures managers analyse the product or service to determine how we can best accelerate success across Africa and the Middle East.

Sector Diversity

Industries we champion across Africa and its Diaspora

From agritech and climate-smart value chains to real estate, logistics and cultural-creative industries, we align global capital with high-quality African opportunities.

We convene decision-makers and form alliances that unlock scale, export pathways and technology transfer.

Digital Transformation Objectives

Aligning programmes with measurable impact

We design programmes that digitise operations, enhance data transparency and accelerate trade readiness. By integrating modern infrastructure with local strengths, we create investable pipelines and de-risk expansion for international partners.

Outcomes include formal jobs, export growth and measurable community impact.
