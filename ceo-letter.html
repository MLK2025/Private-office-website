LETTER FROM THE CEO

Together with Dr. Oseadeeyo Nana Kumi Kodie, we’ve built the Private Office into a formidable catalyst for growth across diverse sectors throughout Africa and its global diaspora.

With more than fifty investments and thirty-five active partner companies supported from our hubs in Accra, London, New York, and Dubai, the Private Office of Dr. Oseadeeyo Nana Kumi Kodie has expanded in lockstep with Ghana’s rise as the commercial gateway to the African Continental Free Trade Area (AfCFTA). Today, Accra links Kigali, Nairobi, and Johannesburg as a magnet for innovation powered by stable democratic governance, science-and-digital growth, and a pragmatic, partnership-driven approach to business.

“We’re shaping new prosperity for Africa, a new horizon for our youth, and a new model of collaborative growth for the world.”

In service of this vision, we work shoulder-to-shoulder with entrepreneurial trailblazers: companies committed to harnessing human ingenuity, creating high-quality jobs, and delivering inclusive growth at scale. If you’re ready to scale across Africa and its global diaspora, we would be delighted to explore opportunities with you.

Yours sincerely,

Dr. Oseadeeyo Nana Kumi Kodie

CEO, The Private Office
