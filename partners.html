OUR PARTNERS

Sector Diversified

Despite coming from different regions and diverse backgrounds, our partners share two core traits—a relentless drive to grow and a deep commitment to excellence. The Private Office of Dr. Oseadeeyo Nana Kumi Kodie partners with forward-thinking ventures that are reshaping how we live, learn, trade, build, and connect across Africa and its global diaspora.

From a rising agritech start-up in Nairobi to a cultural investment firm in London, our partners reflect a rich blend of ideas, sectors, and cultural intelligence. We don’t just open doors—we build the bridges. Through trusted networks, strategic mentorship, and hands-on support, the Private Office creates new roadmaps for sustainable growth and connects our partners to the people, policies, and platforms that matter most.
