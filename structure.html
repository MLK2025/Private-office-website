Realizing Dr. Nana Kumi Kodie’s
Pan-African Vision

About the
Private Office

Founded by Dr. Oseadeeyo Nana Kumi Kodie, the Private Office invests in high-potential ventures across Africa and its global diaspora. We attract leading enterprises to Ghana and serve as a launchpad for expansion throughout the continent. We welcome entrepreneurs who are reimagining agritech, mining, fintech, clean energy, tourism, real estate, and the creative industries. We roll up our sleeves, pairing them with capital, practical advice, and our trusted network so they can grow confidently across the continent and connect with the wider African diaspora. Together, we turn local ambition into lasting, shared prosperity.
