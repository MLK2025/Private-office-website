WHAT WE DO

We secure a direct line to Africa’s key decision makers, bringing you into the rooms where final government and industry calls are made.

Business And Start-Up Mentorship

The Private Office of Dr. Oseadeeyo Nana Kumi Kodie nurtures ventures at every stage, offering top-level governance improvements, public sector alignment, market entry strategies, and growth partnerships. Our advisors shepherd businesses from concept to establishment across Africa and its global diasporas.

We Impart Protocol Knowledge

We enable sustainable market entry for innovative companies into African and diaspora markets. We share deep cultural and business protocol insights, help you identify and engage key customer segments, and provide practical strategies to turn early interest into lasting partnerships. Through our convening power, we connect you with top government and private-sector decision-makers, strengthening your professional presence and credibility across the continent.

We Understand the Business Landscape and Status Quo

The Private Office of Dr. Oseadeeyo Nana Kumi Kodie navigates Africa’s varied business environments—each shaped by political, social, and cultural dynamics—with deep contextual awareness. We build partnerships that respect local norms and traditions while driving strategic progress.
